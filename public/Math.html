Math Modeling

My math class this year is called Math Modeling, taught by Mrs. Burns. In math modeling, we tackle challenging problems and propose solutions to them, learning advanced mathematical techniques along the way. We also participate in a variety of math competitions, such as the AMCs, NEML, and MAML.

As a part of the advanced track for math, I took courses at WPI in C and D terms.

HiMCM 2022

As a part of the Math Modeling course curriculum, we participated in a 48-hour math modeling challenge called the High School Mathematical Contest in Modeling (HiMCM). David Barsoum, Ashwina Bangari, Nicole Plotnik, and I competed in this competition as a group to write a 25-page long mathematical paper to model and solve a problem. At the start of the 48-hour challenge, we chose the problem to model a bee population over time. I really enjoyed this experience because it was a great opportunity to learn about teamwork and math modeling.

Pythagoras High School

Throughout A and B term, we were tasked with writing solutions to math modeling problems using Mathematica. Mathematica is a software provided by Wolfram and can be used for math scripting and computational tasks. The following problem is about optimizing ticket prices in a model high school football game scenario.
